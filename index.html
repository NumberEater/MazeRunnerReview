Review of Maze Runner

Maze Runner is a book, written by James Dashner, that came out in 2009. I rate this book a 5 out of 5 stars. This book was a very good book because it had great characters, a great story, and a good ending. Here are some more of the best things about this book:

James Dashner

  1. The Sci-Fi technology
  2. The dystopian setting
  3. The ability to kill characters in the book while not causing a reader to lose interest.
